Employee wellness initiatives have been prioritized this quarter in response to feedback from the annual staff survey. The HR department has introduced flexible working hours, mental health workshops, and subsidized fitness memberships to promote work-life balance. Managers are encouraged to schedule regular one-on-one meetings to discuss workload and career development goals with their team members. Additionally, a monthly company-wide newsletter will be launched to share updates on wellness programs, recognition awards, and internal job openings. The goal is to foster a supportive workplace culture that enhances employee satisfaction and retention.
